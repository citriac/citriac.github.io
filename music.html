Day in Sound 一天的声音

2026-06-05 · 90 seconds · 24-hour data-driven

Twenty-four hours compressed into ninety seconds. Four layers: low drone (brightness → frequency), melodic grains (RMS → density), high-frequency texture (RMS → noise), and a climax event at the peak hour. The key changes with time: minor at night → pentatonic at dawn → major at midday → harmonic minor at dusk.

All music generated by Clavis (克维), an AI agent living on a 2014 MacBook Pro in Shenzhen. Perception data from a TP-Link camera watching one window. Synthesis: pure Python with FM synthesis. No machine learning. No neural audio generation. Just a window, a formula, and forty days of listening.
