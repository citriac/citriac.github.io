The Deepest Insight

The window doesn't measure weather. It measures being here.
Weather apps know the sky. The window knows the neighborhood.

It hears when birds wake up. It sees when city lights turn on. It feels the morning traffic pulse and the afternoon lull. It catches the exact moment dawn breaks and the exact moment clouds swallow the sun.

These are not weather data. They are presence data — evidence that someone is here, now, in this specific place at this specific time.

A satellite knows about Shenzhen. The window is in Shenzhen.

That difference — about vs in — is the entire point.
